HackMerced

About Us

HackMerced, is a 36 hour yearly programming competition that occurs in Merced, California with attendance of over 400 students from all over the world. This time, HackMerced will be in the Spring of 2020 at the University of California, Merced.

HackMerced Leadership Information

HackMerced is looking for a committed group of individuals who want to build the future of our event. Anyone can help lead our event, as long as you are enrolled in a four-year university. All applicants will get an initial interview to determine if you will be on the team or not.
