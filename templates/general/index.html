About Us

HackMerced is a 36 hour yearly programming competition that occurs in Merced, California with an attendance of around 400 students from all over the world. This year, HackMerced will take place in the Fall of 2018 at the University of California, Merced.

HackMerced Leadership Information

HackMerced is looking for a committed group of individuals who want to build the future of our event. Anyone can help lead our event, as long as you are enrolled in a four-year university.
